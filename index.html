ComoRichWeb Workshops

September 26th, 2012 (that's a Wednesday)

ComoRichWeb will be hosting a workshop night with a focus on getting local developers together for training / learning about specific topics. We will offer 3 tracks from local professionals, with two sessions each lasting 30-45 minutes, allowing attendees to attend 2.

Where? Reynolds Journalism Institute

RJI is located on 9th street, just across from Chipotle. It's on the MU campus. Come in the main entrance on 9th street - we are meeting in the fishbowl room right in front.

Who can come?

Anyone in the Columbia MO area who wants to learn about web technologies. Attendance is free.

Topics

We are offering 3 topics, and you can go to 2 of them over the course of the night. Pick whichever ones you are most interested in and skip the other, it won't hurt our feelings.

  • Backbone.js

    By: Ted Han (@knowtheory)

  • Python / Django

    By: Greg Aker (@gaker)

    Come learn the basics of Python and get started developing websites with Django. At the end of the session, you'll be well on your way to creating applications with this fun and powerful web framework.

    If you'd like to follow along, you just need your laptop with Python installed, a text editor a web browser and be somewhat comfortable with basic terminal commands.

  • HTML5

    By: Brian Grinstead (@bgrins)

    Come learn how to build a nice looking HTML5 site using new of techniques and tools. Covering topics such as HTML5 Boilerplate, Web Fonts, Twitter Bootstrap, CSS3, CSS Sprites, and other stuff!

    If you'd like to follow along, you just need your laptop with a text editor and Google Chrome installed as we will use their integrated developer tools as we go through the workshop.
